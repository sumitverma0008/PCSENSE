Help & Guide

Learn how to use PCSensei effectively

Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Type

Select between Desktop PC or Laptop based on your needs.

Step 2: Define Your Usage

Pick from Gaming, Content Creation, Coding, Office Work, or Student use.

Step 3: Set Your Budget

Use the slider to set your budget from ₹15,000 to ₹5,00,000.

Step 4: Choose Preferences

Select your preferred CPU brand (Intel/AMD) and GPU brand (NVIDIA/AMD).

Step 5: Get Recommendations

Click "Get Recommendation" and our AI will build your perfect PC or suggest ideal laptops.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are the recommendations?

Our AI uses multi-factor scoring based on budget efficiency, usage requirements, and component compatibility. Recommendations are optimized for best value.

Q: Are prices updated regularly?

Yes! Our automated price monitoring system checks prices daily and updates the database with current market rates.

Q: Can I customize the recommended build?

Currently, we provide optimized builds. Custom build editing is coming soon! For now, use the recommendation as a guide.

Q: Which retailers do you compare?

We compare prices from Amazon, Flipkart, Reliance Digital, Croma, Vijay Sales, and MD Computers.

Q: What if my budget is very tight?

PCSensei works with budgets as low as ₹15,000. We'll suggest the best possible configuration within your constraints, including APU-based systems that don't need discrete GPUs.

Q: How do I access the admin panel?

Navigate to /admin.html and use credentials: admin / admin123 (change this in production!).

Pro Tips

  • Budget Allocation:

    For gaming builds, GPU gets ~45% of budget. For productivity, CPU gets more allocation.

  • Future-Proofing:

    Higher budgets (₹150k+) get better components that last longer.

  • PSU Importance:

    We calculate required wattage with 20% headroom for stability and future upgrades.

  • Socket Compatibility:

    All builds automatically match CPU and motherboard sockets - no compatibility issues!
